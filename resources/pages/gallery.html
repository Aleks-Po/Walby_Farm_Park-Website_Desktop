Discover the heart of our farm through its animals.

Welcome to our cozy farm zoo — a place where you can meet our friendly animals and experience real farm life.

Our farm park is home to a variety of animals — from cows and goats to chickens, ducks, rabbits, and more.

Here you can see how they live, grow, and enjoy the beauty of nature. It’s a wonderful place for families — children can meet the animals up close, while adults enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and natural surroundings.
