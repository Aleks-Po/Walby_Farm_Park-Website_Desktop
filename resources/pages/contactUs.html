How to Find Us

Our farm is located in the heart of the countryside, surrounded by fields and forests. We’re just a short drive from the nearest town — close enough to reach easily, yet far enough to enjoy peace and quiet.

By Car

Just a 10-minute drive from Carlisle via the M6 (Junction 44) and A689 towards Brampton. Follow signs to Walby - free parking is available on site.

On Foot

Enjoy a 30-40 minute walk from Crosby-on-Eden along peaceful rural lanes leading straight to the farm.

By Bus

Take a local bus from Carlisle to Crosby-on-Eden. From there, it’s about a 1.5 mile (2 km) walk through the countryside.
